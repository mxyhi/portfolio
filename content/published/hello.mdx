# Hello

Hi, this is the first real post on this blog.

I wanted a cleaner starting point, so I archived the older sample posts as examples and left this one as the only published entry. That keeps the writing space simple: one post, one direction, no noise.

## Why restart

There are a few reasons:

- I want the blog to sound like me, not like placeholder content.
- I want every published post to be something I can stand behind.
- I want the content structure to stay obvious while the site keeps evolving.

## What this blog will cover

Most posts here will likely orbit a few themes:

- building products with AI tools
- shipping software with clear constraints
- frontend and backend engineering trade-offs
- notes from experiments, failures, and rewrites

## A small rule

If a post is public, it should be useful.

That does not mean every article needs to be long. It means each one should leave behind at least one idea, one lesson, or one practical detail worth keeping.

## See you soon

This is only a hello, but it is a real hello.

The next post should be sharper.
